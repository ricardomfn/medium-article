Creamy chicken empada

"Empada cremosa de frango" is a delicious and popular Brazilian snack that consists of a savory pie filled with creamy chicken. The pie crust is made with flour, butter, and salt, and is rolled out and cut into small rounds. These rounds are then molded into small pie tins and filled witha creamy chicken mixture made with shredded chicken, onions, garlic, cream cheese, milk, and seasonings.
